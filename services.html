Services

BELOW AVAILABLE FOR ALL FIRE SURVIVORS LIVING IN THE DIXIE FIRE EVACUATION ZONE THAT WERE IMPACTED FINANCIALLY
Wet/Dry Vacs

We are currently offering free 2-3 day wet/dry vac rentals to people living in Indian Valley. Contact us for availability.

HEATING ASSISTANCE

Firewood, Propane Refills, Heaters, Chimney Repair, Heating System Repairs, etc... Priority is given to emergency cases. Firewood distribution is dependent upon the stock available at our warehouse. The type of wood varies.

Contact us
FOOD PANTRY AND BEVERAGES

2 grocery bags per week per person. Limit two per item on the shelf unless otherwise indicated. Coffee, tea, hot cocoa, spiced cider, water, various cold drinks.

MISC NEEDS

Gently used clothing. Pet and livestock food.

REFRIGERATORS

We have a sign up sheet for people that do not have an operating refrigerator or that have a dysfunctional refrigerator. It is not guaranteed that we will find funding for refrigerators.

Skirting, window insulation, etc...

TENT CAMPING GEAR
